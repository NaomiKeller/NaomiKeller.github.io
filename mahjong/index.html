How to play

Select matching tiles to remove them from the board.

Tiles must have a free space either to the left or the right to be played.

Playable tiles will change color when hovered or selected.

You may shuffle with the button whenever you like, but it may only make it harder.

A shuffle will occur automatically when no more matches remain on the board.

Try to remove all tiles before the time limit to win. Good luck!
